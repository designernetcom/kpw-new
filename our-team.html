About Company

Kolte Pressing Works, established in 1996, is a family-run organization committed to delivering high-quality stamped and machined parts for the automotive, general engineering, and material handling industries .

Exporting 80% to the USA and EU, KPW ensures world-class quality and strong customer trust built over two decades.
