Piston Rod

The Piston Rod by KPW is an exceptional combined product that integrates stamping, machining, and welding processes to achieve superior durability and precision. Designed to handle high-pressure and demanding environments, these rods are engineered for optimal performance in various industrial applications. Their robust construction ensures minimal wear and tear, even under extreme operating conditions.

Crafted from high-grade materials, KPW’s Piston Rods are precision-machined to deliver consistent functionality and reliability. The integration of advanced welding techniques further enhances the structural integrity, making them ideal for industries such as automotive, oil and gas, and material handling.

  • Durable and Versatile
  • Precision Engineering for Longevity

Combined Products
(Stamped , Machined , Welding)

KPW’s combined products deliver a cohesive manufacturing solution by seamlessly integrating stamping, machining, and welding processes under one roof. This integration minimizes production complexity, reduces costs, and ensures unmatched quality in every part.

By leveraging advanced welding techniques, including MIG, TIG, and robotic welding, our solutions ensure exceptional strength and load-bearing capacity. Each component is designed to meet industry-specific standards, backed by thorough testing and certifications for global compliance.
